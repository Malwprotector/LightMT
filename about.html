About Our Tools

LightTune - Guitar/Bass Tuner

LightTune is a precision instrument tuner that uses your device's microphone to detect pitch. It's designed to be:

  • Accurate - Uses advanced signal processing to detect notes
  • Visual - Clear display shows when you're in tune
  • Versatile - Works with guitar, bass, ukulele, and more

Simply allow microphone access and play a string to see the detected note and tuning accuracy.

LightMetronome - Practice Tool

LightMetronome helps you keep perfect time during practice sessions with:

  • Adjustable tempo - From 40 to 250 BPM
  • Multiple time signatures - 4/4, 3/4, 6/8 and more
  • Visual cues - Flashing light to complement the audio

Essential for developing solid rhythm and timing skills.

How to Get the Best Results

For optimal performance:

  • Use Chrome or Firefox for best compatibility
  • Allow microphone access when prompted (for the tuner)
  • Use headphones with the metronome for clear audio
  • Ensure you're in a reasonably quiet environment when tuning
